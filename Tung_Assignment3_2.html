Terry Tao


Mathematical Genius
Studied College math at age of 9
Second ethnic Chinese mathematician to win the Fields medal after Shing-Tung Yau
Youngest medal winner of International Mathematical Olympiad
Born July 17th 1975
Graduated from Princeton
Professor at UCLA Austrialian-American

The story of Tao is quite interesting as at a very young age he discovered his love for math and decided to pursue a career in it. Attending college at a young age, he major in mathmetical theory and famously posed for a photo with well respected mathmetician Paul Erdos. So once you raise an asian kid, remember, math is number one priority.
